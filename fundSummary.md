# AscendIQ — Alignment with AI Horizon Fund Criteria

This document maps how our landing page and site content align with the federal AI fund’s eligibility criteria.

---

## Fund Criteria (Summary)

The fund advances initiatives for a **sustainable AI innovation ecosystem**, including:

- **Workforce preparation** — Prepare American workers for success in an AI-enabled economy, leveraging proven models such as union-led apprenticeships
- **Infrastructure** — Energy resilience and leadership; equitable, affordable access to clean power and water for all communities
- **Governance** — Americans have a voice; AI advancement meets the needs of working people; coordination across state and federal agencies; partnership with industry; technical assistance and community development; workers as part of the solutions
- **Outcomes** — Alignment of private innovation with public good; a cycle of innovation, good-paying American jobs, and infrastructure development
